Playing as a Werewolf
-Roles
  @Killer: The goal of the Killer is to decide together on one villager to secretly kill off during the night, while posing as villagers during the day so they're not killed off themselves.   @Villager: The Villager spends the game trying to root out who they believe the Killers are.   @Seer: Pick one to see at night.

-Timeline
  Ready: When everybody is "Ready!", new game starts. Confirm your role under your local video.

  The Night: The villagers' screens will be covered. The killers silently agree on one villager to kill(sign language is appropriate, or just pointing, nodding, raising eyebrows, and so on). When the killers have agreed on a victim, they click on victim's "X" under the video. All players taking the role of werewolf must agree on a single target.

  The Day: For the day, go around and have everyone introduce themselves (Example: Hey, I'm Matt, and I'm a villager). All the living players gather in the village and decide who to lynch. As soon as a majority of players vote for a particular player to lynch, the system says "Ok, you're dead." If the same number of votes is received, nobody will be lynched. Once a player is lynched, night falls and the cycle repeats.

-Winning
  The villagers win if they kill both werewolves, and vice versa.
